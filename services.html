Our Services

Three key home and outdoor maintenance services. Professional work, competitive pricing, and reliable scheduling.

What We Do

Gardening & Garden Maintenance

Garden tidy-ups, weed removal, lawn cutting and general garden maintenance.

This can include clearing overgrowth, tidying beds and planters, light hedge trimming, leaf and debris clearing, sweeping paths and patios, and general outdoor tidying where needed.

  • Garden tidy-ups and weed removal
  • Lawn cutting and grass management
  • Clearing overgrowth and debris
  • Light hedge trimming
  • Path and patio sweeping
  • General outdoor tidying

Prices are £30–£50, depending on garden size and how much work is required.

Window Cleaning

Crystal clear windows inside and out for residential and commercial properties. Professional cleaning ensures sparkling results and improves your property's curb appeal.

  • Interior and exterior cleaning
  • Residential and commercial properties
  • Frame and sill cleaning included
  • Safe, professional service

Price range: £20–£30

Gutter Clearing

Clearing leaves and debris from gutters to help keep them flowing properly and prevent build-up issues.

  • Leaf and debris clearing
  • Gutter flushing to ensure proper flow
  • Downpipe inspection
  • Safe, professional service

Price depends on the amount of guttering and the level of build-up.

Pricing is based on the size of the area and the amount of work required. A price is always agreed before any work begins.
