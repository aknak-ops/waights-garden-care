Services

Garden Tidy & Clear £35

Light weeding, sweeping paths, and full collection of leaves and debris. Perfect for general upkeep and keeping things neat.

  • Light weeding on beds & edges
  • Sweep/clean paths & hardstanding
  • Collect & bag leaves/debris

Season Refresh £50

Includes everything from Garden Tidy & Clear plus a deeper cleanusing tools to clear weeds from paths, remove built-up growth, and pull vines or overgrowth from walls.

  • All items from Garden Tidy & Clear
  • Deeper weed removal from paths/slab joints (with tools)
  • Remove built-up growth and pull vines/overgrowth from walls

Lawn Mowing Add-On +£8£12

Optional extra with any servicemowing your lawn using your mower and fuel. Great to add when booking a tidy or refresh.
