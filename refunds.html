Refund & Cancellation Policy

No deposits. I keep things simple: you only pay after the agreed work is completed, unless we agree otherwise in writing.

Changing or cancelling your booking

  • More than 48 hours notice: free to cancel or reschedule.
  • 2448 hours notice: small late-cancellation fee of £10 to cover time set aside.
  • Less than 24 hours notice or missed appointment (no access): £20 call-out fee.

If I need to reschedule or cancel

If I need to change the date (for example due to weather or unforeseen issues), Ill offer the next available slot. If I cancel, there is no fee.

Scope changes or extra work

If extra work is requested or needed on-site, Ill provide a price and only proceed if you agree.

Refunds

Because there are no deposits, refunds are usually not applicable. If youve paid in advance by mistake, I will refund the amount in full to the original payment method.

This policy is designed to be fair: flexible when plans change, and clear about short-notice costs that cover wasted travel/time.
