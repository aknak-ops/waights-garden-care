Refund & Cancellation Policy

No deposits are required. You only pay once the agreed work is complete unless another arrangement has been made in writing.

Changing or cancelling your booking

  • More than 12 hours notice: free to cancel or reschedule at any time.
  • Within 12 hours of the agreed start time: £8 short-notice cancellation fee applies.
  • No access or missed appointment: £8 call-out fee (to cover travel and time set aside).

If I need to reschedule or cancel

If I need to postpone the work (for example, due to heavy rain or another unavoidable issue), Ill offer the next available slot or cancel at no cost to you.

Extra work or scope changes

If extra work is requested or required when I arrive, Ill confirm any new price with you before starting.

Refunds

As no deposits are taken, refunds are rarely needed. If you ever pay in advance by mistake, Ill refund the full amount through your original payment method.

This policy ensures I can keep my schedule reliable while being flexible and fair for both sides.
