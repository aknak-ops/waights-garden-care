    FAQs

    Where do you work?

    We’re based in Canterbury and mainly cover CT1–CT5 (Whitstable, Herne Bay, Sturry). Just outside the area? Send a message and we’ll see if we can fit you in.

    What do you bring?

    Everything needed for the job: mower, strimmer, blower, hand tools and bags. You don’t need to prep anything.

    Do you prune bushes or hedges?

    No — we focus on general garden care (lawn cuts, weeding, tidy-ups, leaf clearance, light waste). We don’t offer pruning or large tree work — this keeps prices low and booking simple.

    How long does a visit take?

    Most small/medium lawns take 45–75 minutes. Regular maintenance visits are typically 2 hours. We’ll confirm after a quick chat or photo.

    How do I book?

    Use the contact form, WhatsApp or SMS from the Contact page. We’ll offer the next slot for your area.

    How do I pay?

    Card, bank transfer or cash after the visit — whichever’s easiest.
