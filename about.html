About

Hi, I'm Alex. I run Waights Garden Care across Canterbury & nearby. I keep garden care simple: clear prices, tidy work, and reliable scheduling.

What I Do

I focus on two fixed-price services: Garden Tidy & Clear (£35) and Season Refresh (£50). You can add lawn mowing (+£8–£12) to any booking if you provide the mower and fuel.

What I Don't Do

No pruning, planting, or hedge trimming.

Service Area

Canterbury and nearby towns (roughly 10 miles). Send your postcode and I'll confirm coverage.
