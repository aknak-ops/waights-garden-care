How it works

  1. Get in touch — tell me what you want done and your postcode.
  2. Quick questions — I’ll ask about access, power/green-waste, and the jobs you want first.
  3. Book a time — I confirm a fixed price and a slot.
  4. Job done — pay on the day by cash or bank transfer.

FAQs

Do you bring tools?

I bring hand tools for weeding and tidying. For mowing, I usually use the customer’s mower. If you need me to bring a mower, say so when you book.

Do you take away green waste?

I bag it for council collection. If you need removal, I can suggest options.

What if it rains?

I’ll message to reschedule if the weather isn’t suitable.
