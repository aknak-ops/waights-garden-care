FAQs

Where do you work?

Canterbury and nearby villages.

What do you bring?

All tools needed for mowing, weeding, and tidy-ups. Bags for council green-waste are included.

Do you prune bushes or hedges?

No — I don’t take on pruning or hedge shaping. My focus is mowing, hand-weeding, and tidy-ups.

How long does a visit take?

Light Tidy ~45 mins, Garden Cleanse ~90 mins, Deep Clear up to ~2.5 hours.

How do I book?

Send me a message with your postcode and what you want done. I’ll ask a few quick questions and offer the next slots.

How do I pay?

Cash or bank transfer on the day.
