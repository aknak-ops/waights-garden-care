Policies & Info

Service agreement

  • Tools & mower: I bring hand tools. For mowing I usually use your mower. If you need me to bring one, say so when you book.
  • Scope: routine garden care (no tree surgery or specialist landscaping).
  • Access & parking: please ensure clear access and let me know about gates, codes or parking.

Cancellations & rescheduling

If you need to change a booking, please give 24 hours’ notice where possible. Weather delays are rescheduled promptly.

Waste & recycling

Green waste is bagged for council collection. If you need removal, I can suggest local options.

Privacy

I only use your details to manage your booking and never share them.
