Policies & Info

What to expect when you book — simple and clear.

Service agreement

  • Scope: routine garden care (no pruning/hedge reduction, no tree surgery or specialist landscaping).
  • Tools & mower: I bring hand tools. I can bring a mower — if you’d prefer me to use yours, just say when you book.
  • Access & parking: please ensure safe access and let me know about gates, codes or parking.
  • Safety: pets and children away from the work area; surfaces may be slippery when wet.

Cancellations & rescheduling

Please give 24 hours’ notice where possible. Weather delays are rescheduled promptly.

Waste & recycling

Green waste is always bagged neatly for your council collection. If you need removal, I can suggest local options.

What I don’t do

Tree surgery, hedge reductions, heavy pruning, chemical treatments, or construction/landscaping works.

Privacy

I only use your details to manage your booking and never share them.
